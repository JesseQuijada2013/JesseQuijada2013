About the Car Culture

1. Builds a community

One thing about the car culture is that it builds a community. This community brings people together that show and have the same passion for cars. Whether if the passion is in speed or looks, people can enjoy cars.

2. Builds interests

Many people view a car as just as a moving piece of metal that gets you to point A to point B, but some see it as more. The few see their cars as their own child, they put all their attention to it and want to keep it clean as possible.

3. Gives motivation and inspires

Many people see a nice car the driver has but don't know the hard work needed to get the car. Many people get motivated and work to get the dream car they wanted. Once they finally get the car, other people see and then get inspired to become better to also get a nice car.
